## 🎯 Project Overview
This project targets a critical business challenge for **Databel** (a telecom company): understanding why customers are leaving (**Churn**) and providing data-driven solutions to improve customer retention. 

By analyzing a comprehensive telecom dataset of **6,687 customers**, this analysis uncovers key behavioral patterns, demographic risk factors, and service flaws to transform raw data into operational revenue-saving strategies.

---

## 📈 Key Executive Metrics (KPIs)
* **Total Customer Base:** 6,687 customers
* **Total Churned Customers:** 1,796 customers
* **Overall Company Churn Rate:** **26.86%** *(Industry Benchmark Warning)*

---

## 🚀 The Data Analysis Pipeline
To ensure maximum reliability and business impact, the project followed a rigorous 4-step industry pipeline:

1. **Data Check & Validation:** Performed data cleaning, handled anomalies, and conducted structural sense-checks.
2. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Developed ad-hoc discovery dashboards to test hypotheses and uncover deep hidden patterns.
3. **Executive Dashboarding:** Filtered and migrated high-value charts into clean, grid-aligned, structured dashboards designed for stakeholder consumption.
4. **Insight Communication:** Separated technical analysis into two tailored deliverables:
   * **Detailed Insights Document:** An exhaustive deep-dive analyzing each metric independently.
   * **Executive Stakeholder Report:** A high-level, 3-page presentation mapping out localized problems directly paired with corporate recommendations.

## 🖥️ Executive Dashboards Showcase

### 📂 Deliverable 1: Demographics & Location Analysis
*Focuses on WHO is churning and WHERE the crisis is concentrated.*

<p align="center">
  <img src="images/Screenshot%202026-05-19%20125134.png" alt="Demographics Dashboard" width="800"/>
</p>

* **The Problem:** **Senior Citizens (65+)** represent a critical flight risk with a **38.46% churn rate** (compared to only 18.80% for younger demographics). Geographically, the crisis is heavily centralized in **California (CA)**.
* **The Solution:** Optimize digital touchpoints (UX) for older demographics and deploy localized retention marketing campaigns in high-risk territories like California.

---

### 📂 Deliverable 2: Service & Contract Plan Analysis
*Focuses on WHAT product tiers and contract types are triggering customer exit.*

<p align="center">
  <img src="images/Screenshot%202026-05-19%20125211.png" alt="Service Plan Dashboard" width="800"/>
</p>

* **The Problem:** **Month-to-Month contracts** are highly unstable, driving a massive **46.29% churn rate** and accounting for 76% of total lost customers. Furthermore, customers on Unlimited Data plans who consume **less than 5 GB** churn at double the rate, feeling low perceived value for money.
* **The Solution:** Incentivize contract migration by offering financial promos for shifting to 1 or 2-year contracts. Create a customized low-tier data package to retain low-consumption users.

---

### 📂 Deliverable 3: Operational Customer Support Analysis
*Focuses on HOW internal company performance affects loyalty.*

<p align="center">
  <img src="images/Screenshot%202026-05-19%20125239.png" alt="Customer Support Dashboard" width="800"/>
</p>

* **The Problem:** Customer support interactions reveal a dangerous threshold: customer churn skyrockets to a massive **88% immediately following the 3rd support call**, escalating to 100% on subsequent calls.
* **The Solution:** Implement an automated early-warning system that flags any account reaching its 2nd support call, automatically routing them to a premium, high-priority retention squad.
